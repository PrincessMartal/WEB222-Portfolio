    A little bit about me

    Hello! I'm Princess Martal. Originally from the Philippines, I moved to Canada when I was 14, embracing a journey that led me to explore multiple career paths and passions. I am eager to connect with like-minded professionals and explore innovative opportunities in frontend development.
